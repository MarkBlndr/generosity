About generosity

Helping people find meaningful human connection.

generosity is a nonprofit dedicated to bringing people together in thoughtful, human-centered ways. Through soulmates and mentr, we help people build relationships that are grounded in trust, care, and shared purpose.

Our mission

generosity helps people find meaningful human connection — in love, work, and life. We believe relationships can shape a person’s future, and that careful introductions can open the door to growth, companionship, and belonging.

While we use modern tools where they are useful, our model is intentionally human-first. We listen, we learn, and we guide connections with thoughtfulness and care.

Our belief

“We use technology where it helps, but every meaningful match begins with human attention, judgment, and care.”

What we do

One organization, two offerings.

generosity brings a shared philosophy to two distinct kinds of connection: romantic partnership and mentorship.

soulmates

For people seeking intentional romantic connection.

soulmates supports people who want more than a swipe-based experience. It is designed for those seeking depth, compatibility, and meaningful partnership.

mentr

For people seeking guidance, growth, and support.

mentr creates mentorship connections that can help individuals navigate transitions, pursue goals, and benefit from trusted perspective.

Our values

How we approach every match.

Human-led

We center conversation, listening, and thoughtful judgment in the matching process.

Respectful

We approach every individual with discretion, dignity, and care.

Intentional

We prioritize meaningful fit over speed, scale, or automated output.

Inclusive

We believe thoughtful connection should be welcoming, accessible, and rooted in empathy.
